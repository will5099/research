About Me

I am a postdoctoral fellow in astrophysics at Arizona State University. My research focuses on observations of high redshift sources that are magnified by galaxy-cluster scale gravitational lensing. I received my PhD from the University of Minnesota in 2025, where I worked with Professor Patrick Kelly.
